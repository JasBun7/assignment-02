Community

Study in a focused, respectful space with people doing the same.

Weekly session format

1) Set a goal

Choose something realistic: readings, problem sets, or review notes.

2) Focus block

Work quietly for a set time (ex: 50 minutes), then take a short break.

3) Quick check-in

Share what you finished, what's next, and one useful resource (optional).

4) Plan the week

Leave with a short plan so you don't start from zero later.
